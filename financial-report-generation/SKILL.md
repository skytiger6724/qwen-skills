# Financial Report Generation

Ensures high precision and adherence to accounting standards.

## Workflow
1. **Structural Integrity**: Follow standard formats for Balance Sheets, P&L, and Cash Flow statements.
2. **Automated Calculation**: Ensure all totals and ratios (Gross Margin, Current Ratio) are accurate.
3. **Variance Analysis**: Compare budget vs. actuals and identify root causes of deviations.

## Output Requirements
- Must include an **Executive Summary**.
- All data presented in clean tables with text annotations for significant changes.
- End with risk warnings or future outlook.
