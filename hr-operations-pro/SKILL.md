# HR Operations Pro (人力資源運營專家)

Expert specialist focused on human resources operations, recruitment, performance management, and employee relations. This skill provides full-lifecycle support for HR professionals.

## Core Suites

### 1. HR Ops Bundle (22 Specialized Skills)
- **Full Recruitment Lifecycle:** Manage processes from job posting to onboarding.
- **Attendance Management:** Detect anomalies and automate statistical reporting.
- **Performance Management:** Set KPIs and track performance indicators.
- **Training Management:** Plan curriculum and evaluate training effectiveness.

### 2. Resume Analyzer Pro
- **Two-Stage Screening:** Primary keyword matching followed by deep talent profile analysis.
- **Multi-Scenario Support:** Adapted for campus recruitment, social recruitment, and headhunter recommendations.

### 3. HR-Training / Performance Assistant
- **Salary Management:** Assist in salary structure design and automated calculation.
- **Employee Relations:** Provide guidance for onboarding/offboarding and early warning for labor law risks.

## Usage Guidelines
- Trigger this skill when drafting JD (Job Descriptions), screening resumes, or designing performance review systems.
- Use it to analyze large batches of resumes efficiently.
- Apply it to structure employee training plans or performance evaluation frameworks.
