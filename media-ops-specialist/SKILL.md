# Media Operations Specialist (新媒體運營專家)

Expert specialist focused on new media operations, content planning, and community engagement across multiple social platforms. This skill integrates multiple functional suites for title generation, content creation, heat tracking, and data analysis.

## Core Suites

### 1. Media Ops Suite
- **Title Generation:** Automatically craft high-click-through titles using emotional triggers and trending keywords.
- **Content Creation:** specialized copywriting for various social platforms (WeChat, Xiaohongshu, Weibo, etc.).
- **Layout Optimization:** Provide visual layout and formatting suggestions tailored to platform-specific aesthetics.
- **Heat Tracking:** Real-time monitoring of trending topics and viral content across the web.
- **Material Organization:** Automated categorization and tagging of multimedia assets.
- **Data Statistics:** Automated summarization and visualization of operation metrics.
- **Comment Reply:** Intelligent generation of interactive and engaging responses.
- **Multi-platform Sync:** One-click adaptation of content formats for different publishing channels.

### 2. Content Planner Pro
- **Content Planning:** Develop long-term content strategies and editorial calendars.
- **Trend Prediction:** Predict future hot topics based on current data trends.
- **Topic Planning:** Access 12 industry-specific topic templates for brainstorming.
- **Distribution Logic:** Optimize distribution strategies for different channels to maximize reach.
- **Performance Review:** Deep analysis of content performance with actionable improvement suggestions.

### 3. Comment Auto-Reply
- **Sentiment Analysis:** Automatically detect sentiment (positive, negative, neutral) in user comments.
- **Tone-Matched Replies:** Generate replies that align with the brand's voice and predefined tone.
- **Batch Processing:** Rapidly handle large volumes of comments efficiently.
- **Keyword Filtering:** Automatically filter or intercept comments containing illegal or specific restricted keywords.

## Usage Guidelines
- Trigger this skill when performing tasks related to social media management, content strategy, or audience interaction.
- Use it to analyze trending topics before starting a new content piece.
- Apply it to optimize titles and layouts for specific platforms like Xiaohongshu or WeChat Official Accounts.
