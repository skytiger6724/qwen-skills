# Admin & Clerk Suite (行政/文員套件)

Expert specialist focused on administrative automation, document management, and office coordination. This skill streamlines repetitive clerical tasks through deep office integration.

## Core Suites

### 1. Admin Assistant Suite (38 Specialized Skills)
- **Office Integration:** Automate tasks in Word, Excel, and PPT.
- **Enterprise Communication:** Automated handling of address books, group announcements, and administrative workflows in Enterprise WeChat.
- **Full-Process Coverage:** Manage procurement, reimbursement, and asset management in an end-to-end manner.

### 2. Document Organizer Pro
- **Document Management:** Access 18 document processing skills for automated classification and archiving.
- **Collaborative Agents:** 10 agents specialized in handling complex document-heavy tasks.
- **Quick Command Set:** 35 rapid commands for common document operations.

### 3. Meeting Assistant
- **Meeting Scheduling:** Coordinate attendee availability and meeting room bookings.
- **Minutes Generation:** Extract key takeaways, decisions, and action items from meetings.
- **Follow-up Management:** Generate to-do lists and send reminders to stakeholders.

## Usage Guidelines
- Trigger this skill for meeting coordination, document archiving, or generating office reports.
- Use it to automate data entry and formatting across Word/Excel documents.
- Apply it to manage complex office workflows like procurement or reimbursement cycles.
