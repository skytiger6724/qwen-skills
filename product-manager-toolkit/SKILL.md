# Product Manager Toolkit (產品經理工具箱)

Expert tool suite for product managers to handle PRD generation, requirement management, competitor analysis, and roadmap planning efficiently. This skill streamlines the entire product development lifecycle from research to validation.

## Core Suites

### 1. Product Toolkit (15 Specialized Skills)
- **PRD Auto-Generation:** Rapidly produce Product Requirement Documents based on high-level feature descriptions.
- **Requirement Organization:** Automated classification, prioritization, and backlog management of features.
- **Competitor Analysis:** Systematically capture and compare competitor features, strengths, and weaknesses.
- **Roadmap Planning:** Generate visual, structured product iteration roadmaps.

### 2. User Research Pro
- **Tool Integration:** Bridge with external survey tools like Wenjuanxing or Mikecrm for seamless data collection.
- **Research Frameworks:** Provide 5 professional user research models (e.g., JTBD, Kano Model).
- **Interview Analysis:** Automatically extract core insights from user interview transcripts or recordings.

### 3. PRD Review Helper
- **Automated Audit:** Check PRDs against standard industry R&D specifications.
- **Vulnerability Scanning:** Identify logic gaps, missing edge cases, or inconsistent flows.
- **Logic Validation:** Ensure synchronization between functional flowcharts and textual descriptions.

## Usage Guidelines
- Trigger this skill for PRD writing, competitive teardowns, and user research planning.
- Use it to audit existing documents for logical consistency before handover to development teams.
- Apply it to structure raw brainstormed ideas into prioritized product backlogs.
